Dipali Sarma Bordoloi

Hi

I am Dipali Sarma Bordoloi

Make Up Artist / Hair Designer / Actor

As the first woman makeup artist to establish myself as a professional in Assam, India, I am humbled and honored to have received recognition for my work. I am proud to have been awarded the Best Makeup Artist Award at the prestigious Prag Cine Awards, as well as the Lifetime Achievement Award from Moonlight Media.
